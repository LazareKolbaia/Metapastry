What is MetaPastry?

As the first pastry in the Metaverse, MetaPastry is an entirely unique and interactive ecosystem where you can
play
,
learn
, and
create
your own pastry.

Metapastry is based in The Sandbox Game, one of the
most iconic
metaverses to date.

The long-term vision is to create a bridge where small and medium businesses in the food sector can grow exponentially by connecting with customers around the world.

Furthermore, we are in the process of developing an NFTicket, which will reward our consumers with virtual tickets after each purchase that can be used for coupons, discounts, and even to level up their avatars in the game.
